From bots to consent-first lists

Astroscrape started like many B2B email tools: scraping websites, crawling the internet and collecting addresses. Technically impressive. But not aligned with how we want the internet to work.

We live in Europe. GDPR is not a suggestion here. So we shut that version down and rebuilt Astroscrape as a consent-first way to get B2B email lists: fewer hacks, more respect. Still useful for founders, but better for the people on the other side of the inbox.
