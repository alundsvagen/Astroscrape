Full-time Hybrid Stockholm, Sweden Employee shares

We’re hiring an experienced engineer to architect data pipelines and automate journeys across our consent-first directory. You’ll shape the foundation for reliability, scale, and privacy.

Apply

What you’ll do

  • Design and own database architecture for consented profiles and event data.
  • Build resilient pipelines for ingestion, verification, and export.
  • Automate flows between user journeys (onboarding → verification → payouts).
