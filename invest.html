For Investors

Building the consent-first marketplace for B2B contacts

Astroscrape aligns incentives: professionals control their information, companies access verified contacts, and value flows back to the people who create it.

Mission

Make B2B discovery ethical, efficient, and high-signal by centering consent and rewarding contributors for verified contact data.

Vision

A world where professional data is portable and priced—so people are paid when their information drives value, and buyers engage with intent.

Purpose

Restore trust in outreach. Replace scraping and guesswork with a respectful, transparent exchange that benefits both sides.

Uniqueness

Consent-first directory, self-verified profiles, micro-payouts per download, and compliance by design—creating cleaner data and better outcomes.
