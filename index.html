What is Astroscrape?

A consent-first B2B email directory

Professionals add their own email and choose to be discoverable. Searchers (consultants, agencies, founders) find verified contacts by industry, tags, and region. When a contact is downloaded, the owner earns a small payout. It’s a fair marketplace for attention — and it respects privacy.

Why it matters

Clean data. Real consent. Fewer risks.

Scraped lists are risky and noisy. Astroscrape flips the model: users opt-in, can remove themselves anytime, and your outreach stays on the right side of GDPR. Better deliverability, better reputation, better results.
