What is it?

Industry & Location Specific lists of Real Businesses in Excel Format. Sent to your email inbox.

Why?

The first step of Prospecting is often finding relevant companies. We streamlined this for you. Now you get 100 or 300 businesses in a set location (Sweden or Germany or the UK) and they are real, we have their URLS and some generic emails.

How does this help me?

We know it would be even better if we sold decision makers emails and we know how to technically get those BUT that's illegal in the EU. So we are giving you what is legal and teaching you how to reach out without spamming.

Jump Start Outreach

Research shows Forms on companies websites (Companies of 50 people or fewer) usually go directly to the CEO's inbox. We suggest you start there but the other alternative is to pay someone to take the URLS, use Linkedin to find out who works there and verify their emails with a service like Truelist.io.
